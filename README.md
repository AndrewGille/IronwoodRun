Download and run the HTMl file to play the game!

Hold-to-boost with shrinking fuel. 
B now boosts for as long as you hold it, 
draining a fuel bar in the bottom-left HUD (it glows yellow while burning). 
Fuel capacity drops each level: 8 seconds on level 1, down to 4 seconds by level 5. 
To keep things winnable with less fuel, later levels are trimmed slightly so it's the obstacle density and speed — not an impossible clock — 
that makes them harder.

Random boost pickups. Glowing orange canisters with a lightning bolt bob along the trail (about a 20% chance after each obstacle or ramp). 
Riding or flying through one refills 2 seconds of fuel with a "+BOOST" popup, 
so managing your throttle finger and grabbing cans becomes part of getting a good time.

The yeti. A big shaggy blue-white fellow with fangs appears rarely — a little more often on later levels. 
Every other obstacle punishes contact, but run into the yeti and he grabs your sled and hurls it: a massive "YETI THROW!" launch, 
way higher than any ramp, with a speed burst that carries through the flight. It counts as a jump, 
so the landing pays out distance points — the throws are easily the biggest point hauls in the game. 
He raises his arms in triumph after chucking you, and the generator leaves clear landing room past him.
